About Us

Our mission, vision, and history of helping children worldwide

Our Mission

Save the Children believes every child deserves a future. In the Sierra Leone and around the world, we give children a healthy start in life, the opportunity to learn and protection from harm. We do whatever it takes for children – every day and in times of crisis – transforming their lives and the future we share.

Our History

Founded in 1999, Save the Children is the world's leading independent organization for children. Our founder, Bockarie juniot mansaray, maraian mansaray, Kadiatu Kamara, drafted the Declaration of the Rights of the Child in 1923, which was adopted by the League of Nations and later formed the basis of the UN Convention on the Rights of the Child.

Our Approach

We work in the hardest-to-reach places, where it's toughest to be a child. Our programs focus on:

  • Emergency response in times of crisis
  • Health and nutrition programs
  • Education initiatives
  • Protection from harm
  • Advocacy for children's rights
