Magnificent Construction Website Plan

1. Site Name

Magnificent Construction

This name was chosen to convey professionalism, quality, and grandeur in construction services. It positions the company as a premium provider in the construction industry while being memorable and descriptive.

Optional domain: magnificentconstruction.com or buildmagnificent.com

2. Site Purpose

The Magnificent Construction website will provide:

  • Showcase of completed construction projects with before/after photos
  • Detailed descriptions of construction services offered (residential, commercial, remodeling)
  • Company credentials, licenses, and safety certifications
  • Client testimonials and case studies
  • Contact information and project inquiry forms
  • Blog with construction tips and industry news

3. Scenarios

Target audience questions that the site will answer:

  1. What types of construction projects does Magnificent Construction specialize in?
  2. How can I request a quote for my home renovation project?
  3. What safety certifications does your team hold?
  4. Can I see examples of commercial buildings you've constructed?
  5. What areas do you serve for residential construction projects?

4. Color Schema

The selected color palette reflects strength, professionalism, and growth:

Primary
#2c3e50
Secondary
#e67e22
Accent
#16a085

Color applications:

  • Primary (#2c3e50 - Dark Blue): Headers, navigation bar, footer - represents professionalism and trust
  • Secondary (#e67e22 - Orange): Buttons, CTAs - represents energy and action
  • Accent (#16a085 - Teal): Highlights, borders - represents growth and stability
  • Background: Light gray (#f5f5f5) for clean, professional look
  • Text: Dark gray (#333) for readability

5. Typography

The selected fonts are:

  • 'Arial', sans-serif: Primary font for clean, professional appearance
  • 'Helvetica', sans-serif: Fallback font

Font applications:

  • Headings (h1, h2, h3): Bold version with letter-spacing for impact
  • Body text: Regular weight with good line-height for readability
  • Buttons and CTAs: Semi-bold with uppercase option
